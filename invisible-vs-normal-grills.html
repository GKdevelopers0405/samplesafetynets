Invisible vs. Normal Grills: Balcony Safe & Stylish

Balcony grills are protective structures that are mounted on balconies with the aim of improving safety and security. They avoid accidental falls and offer a safety barrier, particularly in homes where there are young children and/or pets.

In addition, balcony grills act as a security measure against intruders and can be part of the aesthetic structure of a building. Made from a variety of different materials, balcony grills come in different designs and can affect the security and aesthetic value of your home.

Types of Balcony Grills

Balcony grills come in a variety of types, each with its own unique features and benefits:

  • Invisible Grills: These are thin, very strong, and almost blend with the cars from afar since they are made of high tensile stainless steel wires.
  • Normal Grills: These are the normal grills that are made from materials such as wrought iron or stainless steel with visible bars in different designs that help in erecting a barrier.

Purpose of Balcony Grills

The primary purposes of balcony grills include:

  • Safety: keeping residents, especially children and pet animals, from falling off the balcony.
  • Security: serving as a kind of protection against possible intruders.
  • Aesthetics: painting additional designs on the facade of the balcony.
  • Utility: useful as a support for plants and other decorations to be hung outside.

Materials Used for Balcony Grills

Balcony grills can be made from various materials, each offering different advantages:

  • Stainless Steel: This material is very durable, does not rust, and has a very nice look to it.
  • Wrought Iron: Favorite for its strength and ability to be used in decoration, but has the tendency of rusting if not well maintained.
  • Aluminum: Light weight and not easily corroded, making it ideal for use in areas that experience high humidity, such as coastal regions.
  • Wood: looks like a natural finish but is more prone to wear and tear as compared to metal finishes and needs more care.

Balcony grills are a crucial component for ensuring safety and enhancing the visual appeal of your outdoor space. Understanding the different types and materials available can help you choose the perfect grill for your needs.
